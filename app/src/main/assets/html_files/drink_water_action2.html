The Secret Google Experiment that can make you drink more water

Water bottles in Google’s kitchens were simply moved so that they were at eye-level in the cooler while sodas had been moved to the bottom shelves.

This little shift in placement turned out to be enough to increase water intake by 47%, making calories from drink fall by 7% at the same time.

So what can you do? Get a bottle of water, and make sure it is always in your field of vision. You will definitely end up drinking more water!
